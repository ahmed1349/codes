Luxor's Temples & Tombs

One of the most popular countries to visit on the continent, Egypt lies in the northeast corner of Africa, bordered by the Mediterranean and Red Seas. Home to incredible tourist attractions such as the Valley of the Kings, the Great Sphinx,and the pyramids at Giza, it is famed around the world for its ancient monuments and age-old archaeological sites.

Karnak Temple and Valley of the Kings

The Karnak Temple and the Valley of the Kings are among the distinctive signs that distinguish the city of Luxor in Egypt. The Karnak Temple is the largest walled house of worship on earth. The Great Karnak Temple is distinguished by the charming sound and light shows that are held every evening, which is a great way to discover the temple.

The Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are among the most prominent tourist attractions in Egypt, which is one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World. They are three pyramids of varying size,the largest of which is called Khufu, the middle is Khafre, and the smallest is Menkaure.These three pyramids were built to be tombs for the burial of the powerful kings of the Pharaohs in the ancient era.

You also find the Sphinx statue in front of these pyramids to protect them from theft.The statue takes the lion's body as evidence of strength and the human head as evidence of wisdom and reason.
